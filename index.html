J and L Convenience Store - About Us

Welcome to J and L Convenience Store!

J and L Convenience Store was established in 2021 and has been remotely operational for 5 years.

We are located in South, Trinidad, operating 6 days weekly and 13 hours per day. We accommodate nearby patrons with Convenient Parking and Pedestrian Access.

J and L Convenience Store is a family-owned business dedicated to providing our customers with a wide range of high-quality products, afforable prices and exceptional service.

We offer Convenience at Your Fingertips with Your Daily Essentials One Click Away!

Our friendly staff is here to provide you with excellent service and ensure you have a wonderful shopping experience.

We invite you to explore our store and discover the wide variety of products we offer.
